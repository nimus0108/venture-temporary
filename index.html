Venture

Venture is Boston’s first hybrid hackathon + startup competition for high school and college students. We invite students from all over the world to Boston for one weekend for an experience that blends together the arts of hacking and bootstrapping a company. Over 24 hours, 350 participants will build and ship products, create business models and pitch, and ultimately compete in front of a panel of New England’s most respected angel investors for investments and prizes.

Pitch Startup Ideas Hack an MVP Start a company in 24 hours.

FAQ

What is Venture?

Venture is a mix between a hackathon and a startup competition. Participants will have 24 hours to come up with a business plan and build an MVP. Awards will be distributed for different categories.

Where is Venture?

Venture will take place in Boston/Cambridge. Our specific venue will be announced soon.

When is Venture?

Venture will take place on January 9th and 10th, 2016. This is an overnight event so participants will stay at the venue overnight.

How much does it cost?

Nothing. Venture is a completely free event, made possible by our sponsors. Interested in sponsoring?

Will there be travel reimbursements?

We will provide partial travel reimbursements. More details of how to recieve reimbursements will be announced after you apply.

Why should I apply now?

For Leangap's 2015 summer program, under 50 applicants were accepted from a pool of 900 students. Applying early will give you a better chance of securing a spot, as well as travel costs.
